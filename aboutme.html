About Me

I am a goofy, curious, and creative person. I appreciate and enjoy playing music. I used to play drums in a psychedelic metal band called Super Thing.In the tradition of dev boot camp, I need to mention something quirky about me...I LOVE comic books. Image is my favorite publisher and some of the titles I am currently subscribed to include: Low, Manifest Destiny, Black Science, Saga, East of West, Prophet, Drifter... just to name a few. Its hard for me not spend all my money at the comic book store every month.

I have a deep interest in computers, how they work, and how to make them work. I like problem solving and when I learn something new or complete a difficult project I feel like I am doing some kind of arcane wizardry. Its awesome. To further my computer wiz abilities, I am currently attending Dev Boot Camp in Chicago.

My partner Maggie and I recently moved to Chicago from Ann Arbor, MI. We have been exploring the city little by little. Chicago has so much to offer, and I am really excited to be joining the web development community here.
