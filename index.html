About Me

I am a goofy, curious, and creative person. I have a deep interest in computers, how they work, and how to make them work. I like problem solving and when I learn something new or complete a difficult project I feel like I am doing some kind of arcane wizardry. Its awesome.

I currently work as Full Stack Rails Developer at Select Rehabilitation (A large health care company). I've built, maintained, and refactored applications that manage employee payroll, employee training, and labor documentation. I aim to build applications that are clean and elegant- and have a lot of fun while doing it! I love programming, discovering new and better ways to do things, and playing with new technology. I'm excited by design patterns, and sometimes I think I spend more time thinking about writing code than coding.

I currently live in Chicago with my partner Annie. We enjoy going to see live music, reading comics, and playing with our rambunctious cat, Sid.
